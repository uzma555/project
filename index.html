Eid ul Fitr

Blog no 2 | Eid ul fitr

Eid al-Fitr, which translates to "Festival of Breaking the Fast," marks the end of Ramadan, the holy month of fasting. It's a joyous occasion where Muslims come together to offer prayers, engage in charitable acts, and celebrate with feasts and social gatherings. It's a time for expressing gratitude to Allah for the strength and patience observed during Ramadan.
